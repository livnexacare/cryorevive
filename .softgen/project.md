# CryoRevive — Premium Athlete Recovery

## Vision
Elite recovery facility for athletes, bodybuilders, and fitness enthusiasts. Cold plunge, steam sauna, and contrast therapy in a scientific, performance-driven environment. Target: serious athletes seeking faster recovery, reduced inflammation, improved performance.

## Design
Dark theme: elite sports recovery lab aesthetic — clinical precision with athletic intensity.

Colors (HSL):
- `--background: 220 30% 8%` (deep space navy)
- `--foreground: 210 20% 98%` (cool white)
- `--primary: 194 66% 51%` (surgical blue — cold therapy)
- `--accent: 13 100% 61%` (molten orange — heat therapy)
- `--muted: 220 25% 15%` (dark navy variant)
- `--card: 220 28% 12%` (slightly lighter than bg)
- `--border: 220 20% 20%` (subtle separation)

Typography:
- Headings: Rajdhani (700) — condensed, technical, athletic
- Body: Rubik (400, 600) — geometric, modern, readable

Style: High contrast, sharp angles, minimal chrome, data-viz temperature indicators, focused CTAs. Content-forward layout with generous whitespace around action points.
